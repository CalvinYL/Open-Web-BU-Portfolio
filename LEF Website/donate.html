The Impact of Your Donation

Our mission is to create a supportive and cultural connection between youth in Maine and Liberia. Maine high school students from different schools fundraise for a number of Liberian high school students to attend school each year. Our scholarship recipients and graduates are located throughout four of the thirteen counties in Liberia. Because of the instability in the country, the Liberian government does not currently support an effective, free public education system.
